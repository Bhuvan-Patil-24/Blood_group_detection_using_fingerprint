About Print2Type

Project Overview

Print2Type is an innovative project that revolutionizes blood group detection through fingerprint analysis using machine learning. Our system provides a non-invasive, quick, and efficient method to determine blood groups, making it particularly valuable in emergency situations and areas with limited medical facilities.

Technology Stack

Core Technologies
  • Machine Learning: TensorFlow, Keras
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Database: SQLite
  • Image Processing: OpenCV
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap
Key Features
  • Non-invasive blood group detection
  • Real-time analysis and results
  • High accuracy predictions
  • Prediction history tracking
  • Secure data handling

How It Works

1. Upload Fingerprint

Users upload a clear image of their fingerprint through our intuitive interface.

2. AI Analysis

Our trained CNN model analyzes the fingerprint patterns to detect blood group characteristics.

3. Get Results

Receive immediate blood group prediction with confidence score and detailed analysis.

Applications

Medical Applications
  • Emergency blood typing in critical situations
  • Pre-screening tool for blood donation
  • Remote healthcare diagnostics
  • Medical research and data collection
Future Developments

Project Vision

Our vision is to revolutionize blood typing by making it more accessible, efficient, and non-invasive. We believe that our technology can help save lives by providing quick and accurate blood group detection, especially in emergency situations and remote areas with limited medical facilities.

Tagline: Revolutionizing Blood typing with ML

Important Note

While Print2Type provides accurate predictions, it should be used as a supplementary tool and not as a replacement for traditional blood testing methods in critical medical situations. Always consult with medical professionals for final blood group determination in medical procedures.
