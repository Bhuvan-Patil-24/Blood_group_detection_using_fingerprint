About This Technology

This system uses advanced image processing and machine learning to analyze fingerprint patterns and detect blood group characteristics.

The technology is based on research suggesting correlations between dermatoglyphic patterns in fingerprints and blood group antigens.

Our model uses convolutional neural networks trained on thousands of fingerprint samples with known blood groups.
